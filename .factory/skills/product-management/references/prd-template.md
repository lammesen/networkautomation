PRD mini-template (token-light)
================================
1) Problem: who is affected, pain evidence.
2) Goals & Metrics: target outcomes (success, reliability, time saved, adoption) with numeric targets.
3) Users/Stories: P0 must-haves, P1 should-haves, P2 nice-to-haves.
4) Requirements: functional bullets, non-functional (perf, security, tenancy, accessibility), rollout/flags.
5) Tech Notes: models/migrations, APIs, background tasks, UI/HTMX/islands, integrations.
6) Risks/Mitigations: security/privacy, migration, compatibility, ops.
7) Launch: phases, enablement, monitoring, rollback plan.
